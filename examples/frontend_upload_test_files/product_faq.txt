Product FAQ

Q: What does PrivaLoom do?
A: It coordinates privacy-aware local updates and aggregation.

Q: Why are some updates rejected?
A: Validation or reputation checks can reject malformed or risky updates.

Q: When does aggregation run?
A: Aggregation triggers when the update buffer reaches the configured threshold.

Q: What should an admin monitor?
A: Buffer size, rejection rate, reputation trends, and security events.

Q: Can simulation and normal mode coexist?
A: Simulation endpoints are active only when simulation mode is enabled.
