PrivaLoom local training notes

1) Keep client prompts concise when possible.
2) Remove names, emails, and account numbers before sharing text.
3) Use short update batches for predictable aggregation rounds.
4) Review security events before trusting new client updates.
5) Track validation rejections to spot malformed data patterns.

Mini examples:
- Good sample: "Summarize the deployment checklist for branch release."
- Good sample: "Create a short incident handoff note for on-call engineer."
- Avoid: raw credentials, legal IDs, payment cards, personal addresses.
